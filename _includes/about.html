Profile - About

C*O*M*R*A*D*E*S

The show inspired by a Reddit comment and based loosely on true historical events.

Vienna, 1917. five men who were to change the world forever lived within blocks of each other: Adolf Hitler, Josef Stalin, Leon Trotsky, Sigmund Freud, and Josip Broz Tito. This is their story.

FAQs

Who is behind this show?

We are a diverse group of artists, writers, musicians, and researchers united by an interest in history and brought together through Reddit and Discord!

Is this show historically accurate?

Artistic liberties have been taken, but we try to stay as true to these historical figures' personalities and highlight interesting things about them that are accurate to real life.

Is this crowdsourced? How can I get involved?

Join the subreddit and the Discord server! Or maybe don't, you'll keep your sanity that way.
